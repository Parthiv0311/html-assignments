Coca-Cola® Energy

The energy you want. The taste you love. Coca-Cola Energy is the energy drink from Coca-Cola. Each 12 oz slim can contains 114 mg of caffeine. Coca-Cola Energy contains Niacin, Vitamin B6 and Guarana. It gives you the energy you want with a great Coca-Cola taste. Coca-Cola Energy comes in four varieties: Coca-Cola Energy, Coca-Cola Energy Zero Sugar, Coca-Cola Energy Cherry and Coca-Cola Energy Zero Sugar Cherry.

Great Coke Taste

Coca‑Cola Light was created so that you can enjoy a soft drink with no sugar and low kilojoules but with the same great Coca‑Cola taste. Coca‑Cola Light has a unique blend of flavours which gives it the lighter taste all the while still enjoying that refreshing drinking experience. Enjoy Coca‑Cola Light at home with meals, on the go or share it with friends. Best served chilled.

Ingredients: Carbonated Water, Colourant (Caramel), Acidulant (Phosphoric acid), Non-nutritive sweeteners (Aspartame*, Acesulfame-K, Sucralose), Flavouring, Preservative (Sodium benzoate), Trisodium citrate and Caffeine.

DID YOU KNOW ?

Did you know both Coca‑Cola Light and Coca‑Cola No Sugar are both sugar-free and calorie-free?

The only difference is that Coca‑Cola light has a specific, unique and lighter taste.
