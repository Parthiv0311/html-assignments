About Coca Cola

Coca-Cola is a carbonated soft drink manufactured by The Coca-Cola Company. The drink was first invented in 1886 by a pharmacist named John Stith Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia. Today, Coca-Cola is one of the most widely recognized and popular brands in the world, with products sold in over 200 countries.

At Coca-Cola, our mission is to refresh the world in mind, body, and spirit, and inspire moments of optimism; to create value and make a difference.

Our History

Coca-Cola has a rich history that spans over a century. From its humble beginnings in Atlanta, Georgia, to its global presence today, the story of Coca-Cola is one of innovation, perseverance, and determination.

Throughout the years, Coca-Cola has been at the forefront of advertising and marketing, and has created some of the most iconic and memorable ads in history. From the "I'd Like to Buy the World a Coke" campaign in the 1970s, to the polar bears of the 1990s, to the "Share a Coke" campaign of the 2010s, Coca-Cola has always been a leader in the industry.

Our Mission

At Coca-Cola, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products and services. We believe that our success is directly linked to the success of our customers, and we are always striving to exceed their expectations.

As part of our commitment to sustainability, we are constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental impact and improve the communities in which we operate. We are proud to be a part of a global community of businesses that are dedicated to making a positive impact on the world.
